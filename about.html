Our Mission

Empowering Bangladesh's farmers with technology to eliminate middlemen and maximize profits.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, AgriTech was born out of a simple observation: farmers work hard but earn little due to fragmented markets and exploitative middlemen.

We built an all-in-one platform combining finance, market access, and AI tools to give farmers control over their livelihoods.

2024

Launched pilot in Rangpur with 1,000 farmers

2025

Expanded to 10 districts with 50K+ users

2026

Target: Nationwide coverage
